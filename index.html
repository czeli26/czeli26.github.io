About Me

Hi, I'm Crystal, an aspiring software engineer with a passion for AI, problem-solving, and creative technologies. I'm a computer science student at the University of Illinois at Chicago, with plans to graduate in December 2026. I am a member of UIC's Honors College, and a volunteer for UIC's Little Sparks Program, which serves as a resource for parenting students attending UIC.

When I'm not focusing on school, I love spending time with my family and friends. I also have way too many hobbies, including crochet, sewing, pottery painting, or really anything that allows me to be creative. I love trying new things and getting out of my comfort zone. For most of my life, I would not have considered myself a lover of the outdoors, but I moved to Colorado for about a year and a half and found a love for mountains and hiking. I also enjoy traveling. Some of my most memorable trips were when I hiked the Great Glen Way in Scotland with my grandma, and when I took a solo road trip around the eastern half of the US.
